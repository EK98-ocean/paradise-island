Accommodation

We cater to groups of all sizes, from one-bed single rooms to multi-family villas, you can rest assured that we have the perfect room for every group size and budget.

    Our options include:
  1. Singles (sleep up to 2)
  2. Doubles (sleep up to 4)
  3. Suites (sleep up to 6)
  4. Villas (sleep up to 8)

All rooms have views of the sea and all villas have direct beach access.
