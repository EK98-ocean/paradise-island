Paradise Island Resort

Travel Arrangements

Travel to Paradise Island is simple from both local and international destinations!

    You can access the Paradise Island Resort by the following means of travel:
  • Air (3 flights daily on Ocean Air).
  • Road (1 hour drive from Sunshine City, across the Golden Palm Bridge.
  • Sea (2 hour ferry from Sunshine City)

Our travel liaison team will contact you from the moment you book your stay to ensure a seamless travel experience.
